# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in Nexus Core, please report it responsibly.

Do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.

Instead, report the issue directly to:

**Email:** security@reelbigbuoy.com

Please include as much detail as possible to help us understand and reproduce the issue:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Any suggested mitigations (if available)

---

## Response Process

Reel Big Buoy Company will review all reported vulnerabilities and take appropriate action.

While response times may vary, we will make a best effort to:
- Acknowledge receipt of the report
- Investigate and validate the issue
- Determine appropriate remediation steps

---

## Disclosure

We ask that you do not publicly disclose a vulnerability until it has been reviewed and addressed.

Coordinated disclosure helps protect users of Nexus Core.

---

## Scope

This security policy applies to the Nexus Core repository only.

Plugins, extensions, or third-party integrations built on top of Nexus Core may have their own security practices and are not covered by this policy unless explicitly stated.

---

## Notes

Nexus Core is an open-source platform intended for building extensible desktop applications. Users and organizations integrating Nexus Core into their environments are responsible for evaluating its suitability and securing their own deployments.
